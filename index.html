About us

The New York Institute of Music is a music centered private college serving over 1,450 students from around the globe.

Since its founding in 1986, The New York Institute of Music has continued to grow the academic, fine arts, and athletic programs, helping elevate students to their highest levels of achievement. Developing character strength has led our young men and women to serve others in New York and across the globe.
